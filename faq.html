Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything guests usually ask before their first visit.

— reservations —

We recommend booking 1–2 weeks ahead for weeknights and 3–4 weeks ahead for Friday and Saturday evenings. Same-week tables do open up occasionally from cancellations, so it's always worth checking.

Reservations can be cancelled or changed free of charge up to 24 hours before your booking. Cancellations inside that window, or no-shows, are held to a $25 per person fee.

Yes — leave a note in the reservation form or call us directly. We'll do our best to accommodate requests for booth seating, window tables, or a quieter spot away from the bar.

— dining & dietary needs —

Yes. Most of our menu can be adapted for gluten-free, vegetarian, or dairy-free needs. Let us know when booking or when you arrive, and our kitchen will build a course around it — we ask for at least a few hours' notice for more complex allergies.

We don't run a separate children's menu — Noir & Vine is best suited to older children and adults, and most families find the pace of a full tasting evening isn't ideal for young kids. We're happy to prepare a simplified plate on request.

Yes, a five-course tasting menu with optional wine pairing is available every evening for the full table. Ask your server, or note it when reserving.

— visiting us —

Smart casual is the norm — most guests wear collared shirts, dresses, or similar. We don't require jackets, but we do ask that guests skip athletic wear and flip-flops.

Valet parking is available Thursday through Saturday from 5pm. Otherwise, the Cellar Row municipal garage is a two-minute walk and offers a validated rate for our guests.

Our Vault Room seats up to six for private dinners, and we can host semi-private buyouts of the main dining room for larger groups. Reach out through our contact page for details and availability.

Yes — the main dining room, restrooms, and entrance are all step-free. The Vault Room is accessed by a short staircase; please let us know ahead of time and we can arrange an alternative space if needed.

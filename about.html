About Noir & Vine

A restaurant built around two ideas: cook honestly, and pour thoughtfully.

Our Story

A bank vault, a wine list, and a slower kind of dinner.

Noir & Vine opened in a converted 1920s bank building, and we kept the vault door — it's the private dining room now. We started with a simple ambition: build a wine list good enough that people would trust us to choose for them, and a kitchen disciplined enough to deserve it.

More than a decade on, that hasn't changed. We still write the menu around three farms we've worked with from the beginning, still taste every new vintage as a team before it goes on the list, and still keep tables generously spaced so a dinner here doesn't feel rushed.
